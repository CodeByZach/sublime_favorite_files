Version 1.2.2 (2019-03-13)
--------------------------

  This is the last planned release of the 1.x series of the
  package, and is functionally identical to the previous version.

  This is the final reminder that version 2.0.0 is going to be
  dropping imminently, which will:

    * Require Sublime Text build 3297+ (Sublime Text 3.2+)

    * May require tweaks to any customized key bindings you may
      have made using OverrideAudit commands.
